## What we collect

We publish a static website and keep data collection minimal. We only collect optional analytics data when you explicitly allow analytics cookies.

## Analytics consent

Analytics is optional and disabled by default. We only initialize Google Analytics after you select **Accept analytics** in the consent banner.

## How to withdraw consent

You can change your decision at any time from **Privacy settings** in the site navigation. Choosing to decline stops future analytics initialization.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy notice, contact the project maintainers through the repository owner channel.
